Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Rexburg Chamber of Commerce

This name is short and covers all the needed information for the user to know that we're referring to the Chamber of Commerce of the city of Rexburg.

Site Purpose

This website will serve as one of the main ways for business owners in Rexburg to connect to all the resources available to them via the Chamber of Commerce. It'll help strengthen the relationship between all the owners, by keeping them updated of coming events and recent spotlights.

This website will also serve the population of Rexburg and visitors to find the best businesses in town to satisfy their needs. It'll shorten the connection between the businesses and the people, benefiting both parties by bringing the best services to the customers and helping businesses identifying people's needs.

Scenarios

Where can I find a list of all the businesses that are part of the Chamber of Commerce?

Is there any event happening soon that will strengthen the relationship between businesses and customers?

Can I join the Chamber of Commerce by using this website?
