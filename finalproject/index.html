About Scoots

Welcome to Scoots, your gateway to adventure on the captivating island of Cozumel! Picture yourself cruising along scenic roads with the wind in your hair, exploring crystal-clear waters and palm-fringed beaches on one of our sleek motor scooters, ATVs, or rugged jeeps. Conveniently located near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock and the Terminal Puerta Maya, our rental locations are strategically positioned for easy access, whether you're a local in need of convenient transportation or a visitor eager to explore the island's wonders.

At Scoots, we're not just about providing transportation; we're about creating unforgettable experiences. Our user-friendly website is your go-to destination for all things Scoots, offering information on our services, rental process, and island exploration tips.

But what sets us apart is our commitment to helping you forge your own path. While others may stick to the tourist traps, we encourage you to venture beyond, discovering the hidden gems that await along Cozumel's southern and eastern shores.

So why wait? Embark on the ride of a lifetime with Scoots and make memories that will last a lifetime. Whether you're planning ahead or seizing the moment, let us be your partner in adventure on the stunning island of Cozumel!
