Rexburg Chamber of Commerce Memberships

1. NP Membership (Non-Profit):

Support the community without breaking the bank! This membership is tailored for non-profit organizations dedicated to making a positive impact in Rexburg, Idaho.

Price: Free

Benefits:

  • Inclusion in community events and collaborations.
  • Listing on the Chamber's website with a brief description.
  • Access to educational resources and networking opportunities.

2. Bronze Membership:

Lay the foundation for success with our Bronze Membership. Ideal for startups and small businesses looking to establish a presence in Rexburg.

Price: $250 per year

Benefits:

  • All NP Membership benefits.
  • Exclusive access to chamber networking events.
  • Quarterly workshops on business essentials.
  • Basic advertising opportunities on the Chamber's website.

3. Silver Membership:

Elevate your business with the enhanced benefits of a Silver Membership. Perfect for businesses ready to expand their reach and influence in Rexburg.

Price: $500 per year

Benefits:

  • All Bronze Membership benefits.
  • Priority placement in the chamber's directory.
  • Bi-monthly advanced training sessions.
  • Featured spotlights on the home page.
  • Discounts on chamber-sponsored events.

4. Gold Membership:

Experience the ultimate level of support and exposure with our Gold Membership. Tailored for established businesses seeking maximum visibility and engagement in Rexburg.

Price: $1,000 per year

Benefits:

  • All Silver Membership benefits.
  • VIP access to exclusive events and roundtable discussions.
  • Monthly masterclass sessions with industry experts.
  • Premium advertising positions on the home page.
  • Significant discounts on all chamber events and sponsorships.
